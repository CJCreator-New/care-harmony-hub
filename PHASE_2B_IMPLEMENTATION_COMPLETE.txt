✨ ALERT BEHAVIOR
================================================================================

Toast notifications are severity-based:

🚨 CRITICAL  → Red, NO auto-dismiss (persistent until viewed)
⚠️  HIGH     → Orange, 8 second auto-dismiss
🟡 MEDIUM    → Yellow, 6 second auto-dismiss
ℹ️  LOW      → Blue, 4 second auto-dismiss

All toasts have "View" action button that navigates to record detail.
