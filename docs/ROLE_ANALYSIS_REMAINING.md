# Pharmacist Role Analysis

## 1. Role Overview

**Title**: Clinical Pharmacist / Hospital Pharmacist
**Access Level**: Pharmacy (prescriptions, inventory, clinical pharmacy services)
**Primary Function**: Medication dispensing, drug safety, inventory management, clinical consultation

### Strategic Importance
- **Medication Safety**: Final check before patient receives medication
- **Drug Expertise**: Specialized knowledge of pharmacology
- **Inventory Control**: Ensures medication availability
- **Clinical Services**: Provides pharmaceutical care
- **Cost Management**: Optimizes drug utilization

## 2. Core Responsibilities

### 2.1 Prescription Processing & Dispensing
**Key Activities**:
- Review prescription orders
- Verify appropriateness
- Check drug interactions
- Prepare medications
- Label accurately
- Counsel patients
- Document dispensing

**Tools**: Enhanced Prescription Queue, Dispensing Modal, Barcode System

**Safety Checks**:
- Right patient, drug, dose, route, time
- Allergy verification
- Drug-drug interactions
- Drug-disease interactions
- Duplicate therapy
- Therapeutic appropriateness

**Metrics**: Prescriptions filled/day, dispensing accuracy (target: 100%), turnaround time (target: <30 min)

### 2.2 Drug Utilization Review (DUR)
**Key Activities**:
- Review medication regimens
- Identify drug therapy problems
- Recommend alternatives
- Monitor outcomes
- Prevent adverse events
- Optimize therapy

**Tools**: Drug Interaction Checker, Clinical Pharmacy Dashboard, DUR Reports

**Common Issues**:
- Drug interactions
- Therapeutic duplications
- Incorrect dosing
- Contraindications
- Allergy conflicts

**Metrics**: DUR interventions/day, acceptance rate (target: >80%), adverse events prevented

### 2.3 Inventory Management
**Key Activities**:
- Monitor stock levels
- Order medications
- Receive shipments
- Store properly
- Track expiration dates
- Manage controlled substances
- Conduct audits

**Tools**: Inventory Management System, Reorder System, Low Stock Alerts

**Inventory Categories**:
- Fast-moving items
- Slow-moving items
- High-cost medications
- Controlled substances
- Refrigerated items
- Hazardous drugs

**Metrics**: Stock-out rate (target: <2%), expiration waste (target: <1%), inventory turnover

### 2.4 Clinical Pharmacy Services
**Key Activities**:
- Medication therapy management
- Anticoagulation monitoring
- Immunization services
- Medication reconciliation
- Patient education
- Chronic disease management

**Tools**: Clinical Services Dashboard, MTM Templates, Patient Education Materials

**Services Offered**:
- Diabetes management
- Hypertension control
- Anticoagulation clinic
- Smoking cessation
- Immunizations
- Medication reviews

**Metrics**: Clinical encounters/week, patient outcomes, service revenue

### 2.5 Medication Safety & Quality
**Key Activities**:
- Monitor medication errors
- Participate in safety committees
- Implement safety protocols
- Conduct root cause analysis
- Provide education
- Track quality metrics

**Tools**: Error Reporting System, Safety Dashboard, Quality Metrics

**Safety Initiatives**:
- High-alert medication protocols
- Look-alike/sound-alike prevention
- Barcode verification
- Independent double-checks
- Error reporting culture

**Metrics**: Medication error rate (target: 0), near-miss reports, safety culture score

## 3. Key Features & Tools

### Dashboard Metrics
- Pending Prescriptions
- Prescriptions Filled Today
- Low Stock Items
- Expiring Medications
- Drug Interaction Alerts
- Refill Requests

### Prescription Queue
- Priority-based sorting
- Status tracking
- Patient information
- Drug interaction alerts
- Insurance verification
- Quick dispensing

### Clinical Services
- MTM appointments
- Patient consultations
- Immunization tracking
- Chronic disease monitoring
- Outcome documentation

## 4. Operational Challenges

**Workload**: High prescription volume, time pressure
**Safety**: Preventing medication errors, managing high-alert drugs
**Inventory**: Balancing stock levels, managing costs, preventing waste
**Clinical**: Limited time for patient counseling, documentation burden
**Technology**: System integration, alert fatigue, workflow interruptions

## 5. Success Metrics

**Productivity**: Prescriptions/hour (target: 12-15), turnaround time
**Quality**: Dispensing accuracy (target: 100%), DUR intervention rate
**Safety**: Medication error rate (target: 0), adverse event prevention
**Financial**: Inventory turnover, cost per prescription, revenue from services
**Patient**: Satisfaction scores (target: >4.5/5), adherence rates

## 6. Expected Outcomes
- 100% dispensing accuracy
- 50% reduction in medication errors
- 30% improvement in inventory management
- 40% increase in clinical service delivery
- 35% improvement in patient satisfaction

# Lab Technician Role Analysis

## 1. Role Overview

**Title**: Medical Laboratory Technician / Clinical Lab Scientist
**Access Level**: Laboratory (lab orders, samples, results)
**Primary Function**: Sample collection, testing, result entry, quality control

### Strategic Importance
- **Diagnostic Support**: Provides critical test results
- **Quality Assurance**: Ensures accurate results
- **Turnaround Time**: Enables timely diagnosis
- **Safety**: Proper specimen handling
- **Compliance**: Maintains regulatory standards

## 2. Core Responsibilities

### 2.1 Sample Collection & Processing
**Key Activities**:
- Collect specimens
- Verify patient identity
- Label samples correctly
- Process specimens
- Centrifuge/prepare
- Store appropriately
- Track chain of custody

**Tools**: Barcode Scanner, Sample Tracking System, Collection Protocols

**Sample Types**:
- Blood (venous, capillary, arterial)
- Urine
- Stool
- Sputum
- Tissue
- Cultures

**Metrics**: Samples collected/day, labeling accuracy (target: 100%), rejection rate (target: <2%)

### 2.2 Laboratory Testing
**Key Activities**:
- Perform tests
- Operate equipment
- Follow protocols
- Calibrate instruments
- Run quality controls
- Troubleshoot issues
- Document results

**Tools**: Lab Automation System, Quality Control Dashboard, Equipment Management

**Test Categories**:
- Hematology (CBC, coagulation)
- Chemistry (metabolic panels)
- Microbiology (cultures, sensitivity)
- Immunology (antibodies, antigens)
- Urinalysis
- Blood bank

**Metrics**: Tests performed/day, accuracy rate (target: >99%), turnaround time by test type

### 2.3 Result Entry & Reporting
**Key Activities**:
- Enter test results
- Verify accuracy
- Flag abnormal values
- Identify critical values
- Alert physicians
- Generate reports
- Maintain records

**Tools**: Lab Result Entry Modal, Critical Value Alert System, LOINC Integration

**Result Categories**:
- Normal range
- Abnormal (high/low)
- Critical values
- Panic values
- Pending results

**Metrics**: Result entry time, critical value notification time (target: <60 min), accuracy rate

### 2.4 Quality Control & Assurance
**Key Activities**:
- Run QC samples
- Monitor equipment
- Calibrate instruments
- Document QC results
- Investigate failures
- Implement corrective actions
- Maintain certifications

**Tools**: Quality Control System, Equipment Logs, Proficiency Testing

**QC Activities**:
- Daily QC checks
- Equipment maintenance
- Reagent validation
- Proficiency testing
- Competency assessment
- Process improvement

**Metrics**: QC pass rate (target: >95%), equipment downtime, proficiency test scores

### 2.5 Sample Tracking & Management
**Key Activities**:
- Track sample location
- Monitor turnaround times
- Manage sample storage
- Handle special requests
- Coordinate with clinicians
- Resolve issues

**Tools**: Sample Tracking Dashboard, Barcode System, STAT Request Management

**Tracking Points**:
- Collection
- Receipt in lab
- Processing
- Testing
- Result entry
- Result delivery

**Metrics**: Sample tracking accuracy, lost sample rate (target: 0), turnaround time compliance

## 3. Key Features & Tools

### Dashboard Metrics
- Pending Lab Orders
- Samples Collected Today
- Results Entered
- Critical Values
- STAT Orders
- Turnaround Time

### Lab Order Queue
- Priority sorting (STAT, urgent, routine)
- Status tracking
- Sample information
- Test requirements
- Result entry

### Sample Tracking
- Barcode scanning
- Location tracking
- Status updates
- Chain of custody
- Turnaround monitoring

### Critical Value Alerts
- Automatic detection
- Physician notification
- Acknowledgment tracking
- Documentation

## 4. Operational Challenges

**Volume**: High test volume, STAT requests, peak times
**Quality**: Maintaining accuracy, preventing errors, QC compliance
**Turnaround**: Meeting TAT targets, managing priorities, equipment issues
**Safety**: Biohazard exposure, proper PPE, waste disposal
**Technology**: Equipment maintenance, system integration, result verification

## 5. Success Metrics

**Productivity**: Tests/hour, samples/day, result entry time
**Quality**: Accuracy rate (target: >99%), QC pass rate, proficiency scores
**Timeliness**: TAT by test type, STAT completion (target: <60 min), critical value notification
**Safety**: Incident rate (target: 0), PPE compliance, exposure events
**Patient**: Result accuracy, turnaround satisfaction, critical value response

## 6. Expected Outcomes
- 99% test accuracy rate
- 60-minute STAT turnaround time
- 95% QC pass rate
- 100% critical value notification within 60 minutes
- 40% improvement in sample tracking efficiency

# Patient Role Analysis

## 1. Role Overview

**Title**: Patient / Healthcare Consumer
**Access Level**: Portal (personal health information, appointments, prescriptions, lab results)
**Primary Function**: Access health information, manage appointments, communicate with providers

### Strategic Importance
- **Engagement**: Active participation in care
- **Convenience**: 24/7 access to health information
- **Communication**: Direct provider messaging
- **Compliance**: Better medication adherence
- **Satisfaction**: Improved healthcare experience

## 2. Core Responsibilities

### 2.1 Health Information Access
**Key Activities**:
- View medical history
- Review lab results
- Check vital signs
- Access prescriptions
- Read visit summaries
- Download records
- Share with providers

**Tools**: Patient Portal, Mobile App, Health Records Viewer

**Information Available**:
- Demographics
- Allergies
- Medications
- Lab results
- Imaging reports
- Visit summaries
- Immunizations
- Problem list

**Benefits**: Transparency, empowerment, informed decisions, care coordination

### 2.2 Appointment Management
**Key Activities**:
- Request appointments
- View schedule
- Receive reminders
- Reschedule appointments
- Cancel if needed
- Check-in digitally
- Complete pre-visit forms

**Tools**: Appointment Scheduler, Request System, Digital Check-in, Reminders

**Appointment Types**:
- Primary care
- Specialist visits
- Follow-up appointments
- Telemedicine
- Lab/imaging

**Benefits**: Convenience, reduced no-shows, better preparation, time savings

### 2.3 Prescription Management
**Key Activities**:
- View current medications
- Request refills
- Check refill status
- Review instructions
- Report side effects
- Access medication guides
- Track adherence

**Tools**: Prescription Viewer, Refill Request System, Medication Reminders

**Prescription Information**:
- Medication name
- Dosage and frequency
- Prescribing doctor
- Pharmacy location
- Refills remaining
- Instructions
- Side effects

**Benefits**: Better adherence, fewer errors, convenient refills, medication safety

### 2.4 Secure Messaging
**Key Activities**:
- Message providers
- Ask questions
- Report symptoms
- Request advice
- Receive responses
- Attach photos/documents
- Track conversations

**Tools**: Secure Messaging System, Provider Directory, Message Threading

**Message Types**:
- Medical questions
- Prescription requests
- Appointment inquiries
- Test result questions
- Administrative issues

**Benefits**: Convenient communication, reduced phone calls, documented conversations, timely responses

### 2.5 Billing & Payments
**Key Activities**:
- View statements
- Review charges
- Make payments
- Set up payment plans
- Download receipts
- Check insurance
- Dispute charges

**Tools**: Billing Portal, Payment System, Insurance Verification, Statement Viewer

**Billing Features**:
- Itemized statements
- Payment history
- Outstanding balance
- Payment options
- Insurance claims
- Financial assistance

**Benefits**: Transparency, convenient payment, reduced billing inquiries, better financial planning

### 2.6 Health Tracking & Wellness
**Key Activities**:
- Log symptoms
- Track vitals
- Record medications
- Monitor conditions
- Set health goals
- Access education
- Use symptom checker

**Tools**: Symptom Checker, Health Tracker, Educational Resources, Goal Setting

**Tracking Options**:
- Blood pressure
- Blood glucose
- Weight
- Symptoms
- Medications taken
- Exercise
- Diet

**Benefits**: Proactive health management, better outcomes, informed discussions, preventive care

## 3. Key Features & Tools

### Patient Portal Dashboard
- Upcoming appointments
