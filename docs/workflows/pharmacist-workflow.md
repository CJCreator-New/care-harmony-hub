# Pharmacist Workflow

## Prescription Review and Verification

1. Receive prescription from system
2. Verify patient information
3. Review medication details
4. Check for drug interactions
5. Verify dosage appropriateness
6. Check for allergies
7. Review patient medication history
8. Approve or flag for doctor review
9. Communicate with doctor if needed

## Medication Dispensing

1. Confirm prescription approved
2. Verify medication in inventory
3. Select correct medication
4. Verify strength and form
5. Count/measure correct quantity
6. Label prescription bottle
7. Add patient instructions
8. Include medication information sheet
9. Verify patient identity
10. Provide counseling

## Inventory Management

1. Monitor medication stock levels
2. Identify low stock items
3. Place orders for medications
4. Receive new inventory
5. Verify received items
6. Store medications properly
7. Organize inventory by expiration
8. Dispose of expired medications
9. Maintain accurate records

## Drug Interaction Checking

1. Review patient medications
2. Check for interactions with new prescription
3. Check for contraindications
4. Review dosage appropriateness
5. Flag potential issues
6. Communicate findings to doctor
7. Document assessment

## Patient Counseling

1. Verify patient understanding
2. Explain medication purpose
3. Explain dosage and frequency
4. Discuss side effects
5. Provide special instructions
6. Answer patient questions
7. Document counseling provided
