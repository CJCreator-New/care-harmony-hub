## Meeting Schedule

### Daily Meetings
**Daily Standup**
- Time: 9:30 AM
- Duration: 15 minutes
- Participants: Development team
- Format: Virtual (Zoom)
- Agenda: Yesterday, today, blockers

### Weekly Meetings
**Technical Review**
- Time: Wednesday 2:00 PM
- Duration: 1 hour
- Participants: Tech lead, developers
- Agenda: Architecture, code review, technical decisions

**Team Sync**
- Time: Friday 3:00 PM
- Duration: 30 minutes
- Participants: Full team
- Agenda: Week recap, next week preview

### Bi-Weekly Meetings
**Sprint Planning**
- Time: Monday 10:00 AM (every 2 weeks)
- Duration: 2 hours
- Participants: Full team
- Agenda: Sprint goals, story estimation, task assignment

**Sprint Review & Demo**
- Time: Thursday 2:00 PM (every 2 weeks)
- Duration: 1 hour
- Participants: Team + stakeholders
- Agenda: Demo completed features, gather feedback

**Sprint Retrospective**
- Time: Friday 10:00 AM (every 2 weeks)
- Duration: 1 hour
- Participants: Full team
- Agenda: What went well, what didn't, action items

**Steering Committee**
- Time: Friday 11:00 AM (every 2 weeks)
- Duration: 1 hour
- Participants: PM, TL, Sponsor, Committee
- Agenda: Progress, risks, decisions needed

### Monthly Meetings
**Executive Briefing**
- Time: First Monday 9:00 AM
- Duration: 30 minutes
- Participants: PM, TL, Executive Sponsor
- Agenda: High-level status, budget, major decisions

**All-Hands**
- Time: Last Friday 4:00 PM
- Duration: 1 hour
- Participants: All team + stakeholders
- Agenda: Achievements, roadmap, Q&A

## Reporting Structure

### Weekly Status Report
**To**: Steering Committee  
**From**: Project Manager  
**Day**: Friday EOD

**Contents**:
- Sprint progress (burndown chart)
- Completed stories
- Blockers and risks
- Next week's plan
- Budget status

### Monthly Executive Report
**To**: Executive Sponsor  
**From**: Project Manager  
**Day**: First Monday

**Contents**:
- Phase progress vs. plan
- Budget: actual vs. planned
- Key metrics and KPIs
- Major achievements
- Risks and issues
- Decisions needed

### Quarterly Business Review
**To**: All Stakeholders  
**From**: Project Manager  
**Day**: Last Friday of quarter

**Contents**:
- Quarter achievements
- Metrics and KPIs
- Budget review
- Roadmap update
- Lessons learned

## Escalation Path

### Level 1: Team Level
- **Issue**: Technical blockers, minor delays
- **Handler**: Technical Lead
- **Response Time**: Same day
- **Resolution Time**: 1-2 days

### Level 2: Project Level
- **Issue**: Resource conflicts, moderate risks
- **Handler**: Project Manager
- **Response Time**: <4 hours
- **Resolution Time**: 2-5 days

### Level 3: Management Level
- **Issue**: Budget overruns, major delays
- **Handler**: Steering Committee
- **Response Time**: <24 hours
- **Resolution Time**: 1 week

### Level 4: Executive Level
- **Issue**: Project cancellation, major scope changes
- **Handler**: Executive Sponsor
- **Response Time**: <48 hours
- **Resolution Time**: 2 weeks

## Crisis Communication Plan

### Critical Incident Response
1. **Identify**: Incident detected
2. **Notify**: Alert PM and TL immediately
3. **Assess**: Evaluate impact and severity
4. **Communicate**: Notify stakeholders within 1 hour
5. **Resolve**: Execute response plan
6. **Report**: Post-mortem within 48 hours

### Communication Template
```
INCIDENT ALERT
Severity: [Critical/High/Medium/Low]
Impact: [Description]
Status: [Investigating/Resolving/Resolved]
ETA: [Estimated resolution time]
Updates: [Frequency of updates]
Contact: [Incident commander]
```
