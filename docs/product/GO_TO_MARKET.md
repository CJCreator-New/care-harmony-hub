## Target Segments

### Primary Segment: Private SME Hospitals (50–200 beds) — West Africa
- **Size**: ~2,500 eligible facilities in Nigeria, Ghana, Kenya
- **Characteristics**: Running on paper or legacy desktop systems, 20–100 staff, budget-constrained but growth-oriented
- **Decision Maker**: Hospital CEO / Medical Director
- **Budget**: $300–$800/month SaaS acceptable if ROI clear
- **Pain**: Paper-based workflows, medication errors, no data visibility

### Secondary Segment: Mid-Size Private Hospital Groups (200–500 beds)
- **Size**: ~400 groups across target markets
- **Characteristics**: Multiple departments, IT coordinator on staff, compliance pressure
- **Decision Maker**: CTO + CMO + CFO (committee)
- **Budget**: Custom contracts; $2K+/month
- **Pain**: Fragmented legacy systems, high IT costs, compliance audit risk

### Tertiary Segment: Mission/NGO Hospitals
- **Size**: ~800 across Sub-Saharan Africa
- **Characteristics**: Donor-funded, cost-sensitive, international standards required
- **Decision Maker**: Country Director + Program Manager
- **Budget**: Grant-funded; per-seat or per-patient pricing models preferred
- **Pain**: Reporting requirements from donors, paper records, no internet reliability

## Sales Playbook

### Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
- Private hospital, 50–500 beds
- Currently using paper or single-module legacy system
- Has a clinical champion (doctor or nurse) and an operations champion (admin)
- CEO/CMO capable of approving $300–$800/month SaaS spend
- Minimum 3G connectivity in clinical areas

### Sales Stages
| Stage | Action | Exit Criteria |
|-------|--------|--------------|
| Prospect | Website visit or referral; qualify by ICP fit | Contact confirmed |
| Discovery | 30-min discovery call; identify top 3 pain points | Pain validated; budget confirmed |
| Demo | 45-min live demo tailored to their role pain | "When can we get started?" or specific objection |
| Pilot | 30-day free pilot; assign hospital champion | Champion NPS ≥ 7 |
| Negotiation | Pricing + contract discussion | Verbal agreement |
| Close | Contract signed; onboarding scheduled | MRR booked |

### Common Objections & Responses
| Objection | Response |
|-----------|---------|
| "We don't have budget" | "What's the cost of one medication error? One HIPAA incident? Our Starter tier is $10/user/day." |
| "Our staff won't adopt new software" | "We've designed every feature with your clinical staff — let them try it for 30 days free." |
| "Our data must stay in Nigeria" | "We offer in-country deployment options. Our default cloud uses [data center in Lagos/Nairobi]." |
| "Epic/Meditech is the gold standard" | "At $1M+ setup cost, yes. We give you the same security for 1% of that cost." |
| "We tried a system before and it failed" | "Tell me about that. [Listen] What would have made it succeed?" |
