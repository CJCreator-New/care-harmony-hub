# Product Strategy Session Skill

## Overview

The **product-strategy-session** skill enables comprehensive strategic planning and execution for healthcare products. It combines market analysis, product positioning, roadmap development, and stakeholder alignment into a cohesive strategic framework.

**Best For**: Product managers, healthcare startup founders, enterprise digital health initiatives

## When to Use This Skill

Use this skill when you need to:

- ✅ **Develop a product strategy** from vision to roadmap
- ✅ **Align stakeholders** on product direction and priorities
- ✅ **Analyze market gaps** and competitive positioning
- ✅ **Plan feature releases** with medical/clinical context
- ✅ **Define go-to-market strategy** for healthcare solutions
- ✅ **Prioritize requirements** based on impact, effort, and compliance
- ✅ **Build business cases** for healthcare product investment
- ✅ **Navigate regulatory constraints** (HIPAA, FDA, clinical workflows)
- ✅ **Identify revenue models** appropriate for healthcare

## Skill Methodology

### Phase 1: Discovery & Analysis
- Market landscape assessment (TAM, SAM, SOM)
- Competitive analysis for healthcare solutions
- Stakeholder interviews and requirements gathering
- Regulatory/compliance environment mapping
- Clinical workflow analysis and pain points

### Phase 2: Strategy Definition
- Product vision and mission articulation
- Positioning and messaging strategy
- Core value proposition for users/organizations
- Success metrics and KPIs
- Risk assessment and mitigation planning

### Phase 3: Execution Planning
- Feature prioritization framework (impact/effort/risk)
- Phased roadmap with clinical milestones
- Go-to-market timeline and channels
- Resource allocation and budgeting
- Stakeholder communication plan

### Phase 4: Implementation & Governance
- Quarterly strategy reviews
- Market feedback incorporation
- Roadmap adjustments based on clinical feedback
- Success metrics tracking
- Post-launch optimization

## Healthcare Context

This skill understands:

- **Clinical Workflows**: EHR integration, clinical decision support, care coordination
- **Regulatory**: HIPAA compliance, FDA 510(k) classification, state licensing requirements
- **Stakeholders**: Clinicians, hospital administrators, healthcare IT, patients, payers
- **Market Dynamics**: Healthcare IT adoption cycles, reimbursement models, consolidation trends
- **Implementation**: Enterprise software deployment, clinical staff training, change management

## Output Format

The skill produces comprehensive strategy documents:

1. **Executive Summary** — Market opportunity, positioning, 90-day plan
2. **Strategic Framework** — Vision, mission, values, success metrics
3. **Market Analysis** — TAM/SAM/SOM, competitive landscape, differentiation
4. **Roadmap** — Phased feature prioritization with clinical validation
5. **Go-to-Market** — Launch channels, pricing, customer acquisition
6. **Financial Projections** — Revenue model, pricing strategy, unit economics
7. **Risk Analysis** — Regulatory, market, execution risks with mitigation
8. **Stakeholder Plan** — Communication, buy-in, governance structure
